How to Play Cards of Kursk:

To play you will need to create an account. Ones its created it will grant you access to your very own garage and by clicking on the username displayed on the header of the page you will access it.

Garage

The garage will display the cards you own and by clicking on them they will be send to the Trading Park.

Careful!! Ones you send a card to the Trading Park you arenÂ´t going to be able to retrieve it, so think carefully if you really want to send it.

The cards on the trading park will be highlighted with a blue border.

Trading Park:

Here is where magic happens!! In it will be displayed all the cards all the users have push to trade, even yours.

Your cards will be highlighted with a red border, you would not be able to take them back.

By having cards on the Trading Park you will be able to pick cards of other users. The picked cards will be send to your garage and one of your cards on trading will be selected and send to the garage of the original user of the picked card.
